### [BRAND STATEMENT — Body text]

From the outset, form and function were critical elements in Mavericks design philosophy.

The aim was to produce a 21st century rock guitar, utilizing Mavericks patented " Evolution " volume and tone system. Collaboration with the renowned guitar designer Trevor Wilkinson, enabled Maverick to create its definitive style.

All materials are selected to enhance both the guitars feel and tone. Hand crafted bodies are then coupled with three piece Maple and Bubinga necks, which employ the use of a double truss rod, allowing total accuracy of adjustment.

At the heart of Mavericks sound are the 'A' series pick-ups. The natural sound of the F-1 Humbucker is ideally suited for the guitars needs, where as the demands of the X-1 were higher output and darker tones. The B-1 bass varies as it dedicates one " Evolution " roller to each of its three way active pick-ups. All pick-ups are voiced and sound tested in the U.K.

A two year extended warranty supported through our appointed dealer / distributor network, establishes Mavericks commitment to quality guitar building.
