## PAGE 2 — The Ethos of Evolution

### [LOGO — Top left]

*Maverick*

### [HEADLINE — Large bold/display type]

**THE ETHOS OF EVOLUTION**

### [BODY COPY]

At Maverick we have one simple objective: to build some of the most desirable guitars in the world. In 1997 we were presented with a unique opportunity by our close friend Trevor Wilkinson. Trevor, one of the world's most renowned designers introduced us to a very small custom workshop in Korea. We quickly realised the massive potential due to the incredible level of workmanship and hand crafting skills on offer. We then set about researching the requirements of contemporary players from around the world. There was a genuine belief after our extensive studies that improvements could, and should, be made in many areas of existing manufacturing techniques.

In late 2000 the first Maverick guitars hit the streets. It was quite astonishing; players, dealers, magazines and competitors alike were surprised by the acute attention to detail and refreshing design application.

Maverick's patented Evolution roller volume and tone system presented for the first time an alternative method of controlling the guitars output and tonal variations. Supreme playability and incredible build quality received instant acclaim.

## PAGE 3 — F-1 (Evolution Series)

### [SECTION HEADER — Top right]

**EVOLUTION series**

### [SERIES LOGO]

*Maverick* EVOLUTION **F1**

### [COLOUR LABELS — Miniature guitar images, top centre]

Tribal Red | Dayglow | Desert Storm | Cosmos Black

### [COLOUR LABEL — Main guitar image]

Lunar Blue

### [BODY COPY]

The F1 was the birthplace of the Maverick electric guitar. An incredible amount of effort goes into every instrument we produce in order to ensure an effortless playing experience. Select Maple and Bubinga are matched with AAA grade Indian Rosewood to hand build every Maverick neck. This is then coupled to humidity controlled basswood bodies and loaded with a pair of our 'A' series humbuckers. Pick-up selection is via a 3 way toggle switch offering the classic twin humbucking tones.

## PAGE 4 — F-2 (Evolution Series)

### [SERIES LOGO]

*Maverick* EVOLUTION **F-2**

### [COLOUR LABELS]

Fireburst | Tobacco Sunburst

### [BODY COPY]

The Maverick F2 guitar possesses great natural string resonance directly resulting from the American tonewoods and thru-body stringing. A two piece bookmatched Alder body has been selected for its legendary tone and classic looks. Tonal variation is increased by utilising Maverick's custom 5 way switch and a pair of tapped 'A' series humbuckers. This guitar is capable of producing everything from out and out rock, through to the subtlest single coil neck tones. The F2 offers a blend of stunning looks and expanded sonic capabilities, combined with Maverick's exquisite playability.

Providing what is still regarded as today's classic pick-up configuration, 2 'A' series humbuckers are perfectly matched to our overwound "Alnico V" single coil. Tribal Red | Lunar Blue

### [COLOUR LABEL — Main guitar image]

From its first sighting anyone can see that this is an out-and-out rock guitar. The body is constructed using the finest 2 piece American Alder and is equipped with a matched pair of custom X-Treme pick-ups.

## PAGE 10 — F D-Tox (D-Tox Series)

### [SERIES LOGO]

*Maverick* D-TOX **F D-tox**

### [COLOUR LABEL — Miniature guitar image]

Desert Storm

### [COLOUR LABEL — Main guitar image]

Cosmos Black

### [BODY COPY]

As music changes so does the method of producing it. Developed in conjunction with some of our existing players the criteria was simplicity and usability. The D-Tox has a very specific purpose, one that gives the player exactly what is required yet removes everything surplus to their needs. A single volume and 3 way toggle switch control Maverick's new active pick-up system. When plugged in, the benefit of our on board active circuitry becomes very apparent. On clean amp settings the guitar has a wonderful, crisp transparency that really accentuates the notes.

When in the overdrive mode the pick-ups produce great output while retaining accurate definition at all times.

## PAGE 12 — Chaos 1 & Chaos 2 (Century Series)

### [SERIES LOGOS]

*Maverick* CENTURY **Chaos 1**
*Maverick* CENTURY **Chaos 2**

### [COLOUR LABELS — Miniature guitar images, top]

Chaos 1: Lunar Blue | Gun Metal | Cosmos Black
Chaos 2: Lunar Blue | Cosmos Black

### [COLOUR LABELS — Main guitar images]

Chaos 1: Tribal Red
Chaos 2: Gun Metal

### [BODY COPY]

This range presented a challenging brief to the designers at Maverick. Specifically to produce an instrument without any compromise on quality. The concept was initiated during conversations between Chris Ingham, editor of Metal Hammer Magazine and ourselves. Chris knows that Maverick build beautiful guitars, but believes that many of his readers may aspire to own a Maverick but cannot achieve the levels of our existing range.

The Century Series guitars are hand built by our craftsmen, in the exact same manner as our Evolution and D-Tox Series instruments. Solid tonewoods along with maple and rosewood are employed throughout the range. These guitars offer professional build quality and great playability for guitarists everywhere.

To confirm our commitment to the range we installed our new Evolution Series X-1 pick-ups. Wired via a Custom 5 way selector switch, outstanding humbucking and single coil sounds are available on demand.

**Available in either Floyd Rose or Fixed Bridge options.**

## PAGE 13 — Species 1 & Species 2 (Century Series)

### [SECTION HEADER — Top right]

**CENTURY series**

### [SERIES LOGOS]

*Maverick* CENTURY **Species 2**
*Maverick* CENTURY **Species 1**

### [BODY COPY]

Maverick's 6 and 7 string Species guitars were conceived and developed with one thing in mind - unrivalled sustain.

Fundamental to our design philosophy was the use of solid tonewood bodies and the application of a glued in neck. The direct coupling of neck to body delivers improved resonance to the guitars natural sound. Maverick's recessed tune'o'matic bridge and thru-body stringing satisfy the critical element of stable tuning. At the heart of our Species guitars sit the Evolution series X-1 pick-ups. The high output and precise tone are a perfect blend for these instruments. Wiring is via our custom 5 way selector switch, offering authoritative humbucker and single coil tones.

**Species 6 string fitted with 10-52s**
**Species 7 string fitted with 10-60s**

All Species guitars are available in Cosmos Black

## PAGE 14 — Nemesis S4 & S5 Basses (Nemesis Series)

### [SECTION HEADER — Top left]

**NEMESIS series**

### [SERIES LOGOS]

*Maverick* NEMESIS **S4**
*Maverick* NEMESIS **S5**

### [COLOUR LABELS — Miniature images]

Transparent Black | Natural | Transparent Black

### [COLOUR LABELS — Main guitar images]

S4: Fireburst
S5: Natural

### [BODY COPY]

Many of today's new wave basses have become very small and nimble. Maverick's ambition was to build a new age rock bass with the same critique as the classics. Precisely: to have the correct body mass to produce an instrument capable of great sustain and natural resonance. Maverick disregarded manufacturing costs and selected American bookmatched Swamp Ash as the ultimate body material. This commitment was fully rewarded with an incredibly responsive bass.

In association with our great friend Trevor Wilkinson, we designed a very dynamic 3 band active EQ. When this is used in conjunction with Maverick's Evolution volume and tone system the Nemesis basses have limitless capabilities.
