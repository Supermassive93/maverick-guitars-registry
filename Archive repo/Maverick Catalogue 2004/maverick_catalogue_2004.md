*f-3*

**Maverick F-3**

### [BODY COPY]

Styled around our F-1 model, the 2004 F-3 surpassed even our own expectations in its latest specification. The Bookmatched U.S Alder Body and one piece Canadian carved Maple Neck are fully complimented with a range of translucent finishes, and then further enhanced with Neck and Headstock bindings. With the added advantage of our MSC Alnico 'V' Single Coil and 5 way selector for Increased Tonal capabilities, this guitar is both beautiful and versatile.

## PAGE 6 — S-4/5 Bass

### [MODEL IDENTIFIER — Top left, headstock silhouette graphic]

*S-4/5*

### [MODEL HEADER]

**Maverick S-4/5**

### [BODY COPY]

Mavericks 4 and 5 string Basses are manufactured from some of the finest materials available. Both models are constructed from a 2 x Piece Bookmatched U.S Alder Body which is then in turn fitted with our Hand shaped Re-enforced Canadian Rock Maple and Bubinga Neck. Limitless sound options are instantly on hand courtesy of the twin Nemesis Pick-ups wired via Maverick's powerful Onboard 3 Band Active E.Q system.

### [LEGAL SMALL PRINT — Bottom of page]

Maverick Evolution Volume & Tone System is a registered design No. 2096867. Evolution® is a registered trademark of Dimarzio Inc. with which Maverick Guitars is not affiliated.
