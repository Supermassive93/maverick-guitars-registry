**BARRY GEORGE STOCK**

> **Note:** Registered in the name of Barry George Stock — co-founder of Maverick Guitars — not Mark James or Maverick Guitars Ltd. This is significant as it establishes personal IP ownership by Stock rather than the company.

### Key Notes

| Note | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Applicant identity** | Registered in name of Barry George Stock (co-founder), not Mark James or Maverick Guitars Ltd. — establishes personal IP ownership |
| **Timing** | Applied and granted October/November 2000 — just before first guitars hit market in late 2000 |
| **Scope of novelty** | Dual claim: whole article AND specific Evolution control area — broader protection than control system alone |
| **Cross-references in Maverick literature** | Confirmed in 2001 brochure, 2002 catalogue, 2004 catalogue small print as design No. 2096867 |
| **Note re Evolution® trademark** | Evolution® is separately a registered trademark of Dimarzio Inc. — Maverick's registered design covers the physical design, not the name |
