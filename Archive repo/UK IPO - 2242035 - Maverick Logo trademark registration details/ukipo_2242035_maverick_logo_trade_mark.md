### [LOGO — Trade mark image, cursive script]

The registered trade mark is a stylised cursive script rendering of the word "Maverick". The lettering is fluid and handwritten in style, with an extended descending flourish beneath the word forming an underline/sweep. The capital "M" has a distinctive loop at the top. The overall mark is monochromatic (black on white). This is the headstock logo used on all Maverick Guitars instruments.

### Key Analytical Notes

| # | Observation | Significance |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Trade mark filed 09 August 2000 — the same month the IPO registered design 2096867 (Evolution V&T System) was also being progressed | Confirms August–October 2000 as the period of Maverick's full IP registration activity, immediately before first guitars reached market in late 2000 |
| 2 | Original proprietor address (Park House, Sopworth, Chippenham, Wiltshire, SN14 6PR) does not match either the Gen 1 trading address (Tetbury, Gloucestershire) or the Gen 2 trading address (Grimsby) | Sopworth/Chippenham is likely the registered company address or directors' home address, not a trading or warehouse address; warrants further investigation |
| 3 | Trade mark assigned from Maverick Guitars Limited to Mark Vincent James personally on 10 April 2006 — effective 17 months before the company closure announced September 2007 | Directly parallels the registered design expiry pattern (designs 3001705 and 3001706 expired March 2007 without renewal). The brand's most visible asset — the logo — was extracted from the company into personal ownership before closure, suggesting deliberate IP preservation by James personally |
| 4 | Barry George Stock is absent from this trade mark entirely — neither as co-applicant nor in the assignment | Contrasts with registered designs 3001705 and 3001706 (filed jointly, Stock + James) but aligns with the 2096867 anomaly (filed by Stock alone). The trade mark was always a James asset, not a Stock asset |
| 5 | Mark Vincent James renewed the trade mark in 2010 and again in 2020 (after a 9-day lapse), with next renewal due 2030 | As of May 2026, the "Maverick" cursive script logo remains a live, actively maintained registered trade mark in the sole name of Mark Vincent James. Any commercial use of the mark requires his authorisation |
| 6 | The mark covers Class 15 (Musical instruments) only | No broader registration (clothing, accessories, etc.) — the protection is limited strictly to the instrument/music hardware category |
| 7 | The Grimsby address on the current proprietor record (Units 6 & 7 South Quay Business Centre) matches the Gen 2 Maverick trading address | Confirms Mark James has operated from Grimsby continuously from at least the Gen 2 period through to the present day |
